Breath of the Wild Review


Breath of the Wild is an open-world RPG about a protagonist named Link, who has to save the princess named Zelda. Link has to fight Calamity Ganon in order to free Zelda who has been supressing Ganon for hundreds of years. You will have to help villagers, fight monsters, and gear up in order to become strong enough to fight Ganon.



Characters

Link

Link is a Hylian, which is a race with elfish features such as long, pointed ears. He has blond hair, blue eyes, and is canonically described as handsome. He is physically strong and is known for his stoic silent personality. Link does not have any dialogue in the game. Instead, he communicates through grunts, yells, and humming sounds.

Link is the main protagonist of Breath of the Wild. At the beginning of the game, he wakes up in the Shrine of Resurrection after being asleep for 1,000 years. Shortly after waking up, he hears Princess Zelda’s voice urging him to regain his strength and recover his lost memories so he can rescue her and defeat Calamity Ganon. In order to do this, Link must explore Hyrule, gear up and exploring ancient ruins to grow powerful enough to defeat the Calamity.

Before the Great Calamity 1000 years ago, Link served as Princess Zelda’s personal guard. Together with the Champions, they fought to stop Calamity Ganon but ultimately failed, which made Link became severely injured. To save him and the world, Zelda placed Link inside the Shrine of Resurrection to heal, knowing he would awaken 1,000 years later to defeat Ganon. When Link awakens at the start of the game, his memories are gone and he must journey across Hyrule to regain his strength and memories, confront Calamity Ganon, and free Zelda, who is holding him back within Hyrule Castle.

Zelda

Zelda is the deuteragonist of the story, who is a dasmel in distress that has sacrificed her body in order to keep the antagonist, Calmity Ganon sealed away for 1000 years. Struggling to unlocked her power she has sealed Ganon away for 1000 years, buying time for Link to wake up and slay Calmity Ganon. She is a Hylian and was the princess of Hyrule 1000 years ago before Calamity Ganon had destroyed the castle.
