Breath of the Wild Review


Breath of the Wild is an open-world RPG about a protagonist named Link, who has to save the princess named Zelda. Link has to fight Calamity Ganon in order to free Zelda who has been supressing Ganon for hundreds of years. You will have to help villagers, fight monsters, and gear up in order to become strong enough to fight Ganon.



Characters

Link

Link is the main protagonist in Breath of the Wild. His character does not have any voicelines, and the sounds he makes includes grunts, yells, humming, and clapping. He is considered a Hylian, which is a race that posses elfish features such as long pointy ears. He also has blue eyes and blond hair and is cannonically considered handsome. He is also quite strong and has a stoic and silent personality.
