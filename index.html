Breath of the Wild Review

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a game where you play as Link to save Princess Zelda. It came out on Nintendo Switch in 2017. You can explore a big world and do quests in any order.

Opening Scene

Characters

Link

Link is the hero. He wakes up after sleeping for 100 years and has to save Hyrule and Zelda from Calamity Ganon.

Zelda

Zelda is a princess with special powers. She helps keep Ganon away while Link gets stronger.

Calamity Ganon

Calamity Ganon is the bad guy. He almost destroyed Hyrule, and Link must defeat him to save the world.

Ending Scene

At the end, Link defeats Ganon with the help of his friends and saves Zelda.

Why I Like This Game

This game is one of my favorites because it lets you explore and enjoy nature. It feels like a real, living world.
